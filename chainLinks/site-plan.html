Overview

Purpose

Give information about the characters in the comic "Linked Universe" based off the Legend of Zelda. Also allow users to play a fun mini game.

Audience

Anyone who is interested in a theoretical crossover of Legends of Zelda or the games. Also anyone who wants to play with battle cards.

Dynamic elements

There will be a search feature to find specific characters. Once on the character page you can click on an option that brings up a card of the character and a random enemy. You can then press buttons on the card to deal damage to the enemy character. Potentially also include a framework for the character pages that just loads data about the characters from an object/array.

About

About the site: This site allows you to search up characters based off of the comic "Linked Universe", a fan made comic combining the Legends of Zelda games into 9 Links who travel through time adventuring together. Once you find a Link you like, you can click to open their character page, which will give a bit more information about them and allow you to battle. Once you click the battle button a random enemy specific to the Link you're playing as will appear, and your character card will attack theirs with one of their special skills.
